Entrepreneurs-in-Residence Initiative

Support entrepreneurs hands-on in startup hubs in six different countries.

Join us!

What´s the Entrepreneurs-in-Residence Initative?

We place internationally experienced and well-connected entrepreneurs/startup supporters in upcoming ecosystems in the Western Balkans, Peru and Vietnam with the goal to support building an entrepreneurial community, the design of programs for startups and thus contribute to foster respective ecosystems for more entrepreneurs to be successful.

Why do we want you to join?

  • You are a founder, executive or investor with experience in building scalable ventures
  • You are pro-active and engaged
  • You are keen to share you knowledge and experiences in supporting startups
  • You can make valuable global connections with international accelerators
  • You have a high degree of community spirit and a passion for entrepreneurship and change.
  • You are interested in supporting to create a business culture in emerging markets.

What are the destinations for you?

We are working with the leading startup organizations, innovation hubs and incubators in the following countries: Albania, Bosnia & Herzegovina, Macedonia, Peru, Serbia and Vietnam. So just choose!

What is the deal?

You contribute your time - we take care of the expenses

If you're interested in giving back to entrepreneurs we will provide:
  • Roundtrip Airfare
  • Accommodation
  • Logistical and individual support on the ground
  • Office space in some of the best working spots at your destination
In return we expect:
  • that you dedicate ~20 hours per week to activley work on business development with players throughout all levels of the ecosystem.
    • The EiR program is pro-bono and we do not pay fees.

Who will you work with?

Managers of acclerators, incubators and other business support organizations who support local startups to build and sustain their venture.
